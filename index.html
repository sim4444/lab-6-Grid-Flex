Life Of A BCIT Student

My Campus Life Experience

My name is Simran and I am a Term 1 student. Getting admission in BCIT's CIT program is really a big achievement for me because BCIT has set very high standards of approving admission. I am enrolled in 7 courses right now and all the courses are highly effective in learning useful technical skills. My fellow classmates are actually helpful and always try to solve problems together to work as a team. I am having an amazing experience of learning skills and establishing great friendships with my peers. Three of my top favourite courses are:

  1. Technical Maths for IT
  2. Fundamental Web Technologies
  3. Scripting for IT
